Fringe-like

string of pendants

Inv. Аap 119, Бз 83, П 135, D 5992
Material: Gold; weight 4.97 g.
Technique: Forging
Size: Length 16.6 cm.; length of a loop 0.5-0.6 cm. Width of the wire 0.03 cm

A string of short, loop-like pendants made of thin, hammered four-sided wire mounted on a copper wire, plus four-minute beads. The whole produces the effect of a fringe.

Beads are scorched. Pendants are bent and soiled.
