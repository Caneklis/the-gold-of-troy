String

of beads with pendant

Inv. no. Aap 89, Бз 24, П 61, A 5955
Material: Gold; weight 21.42 g
Technique: Drawing, soldering, embossing
Size: Length 37.0 cm

A string of beads of various shapes. There are very few minute beads and more large beads, including smooth and toothed round beads; square disks; barrel-shaped ribbed; hexagonal truncated biconical beads; and square ones with incisions on the sides. There are two smooth barrel-shaped solid beads, and tubular two-winged beads, one tubular tetrahedral, and two spindle-shaped beads. Also, there is one mushroom-shaped pendant.
