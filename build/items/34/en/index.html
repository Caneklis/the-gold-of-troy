Stud-shaped

object

Inv. Аар 67, Бз 68, П 113, А 5933
Material: Gold; weight 0.99 g
Technique: Forging, soldering, graining
Size: Length 1.24 cm; diameter of heads 0.75-0.76 cm; diameter of the hollow tube 0.18 cm

Double stud consisting of two similar joined elements. Each consists of a hollow hemisphere, one having a solid wire stem soldered to its interior surface. The other has a hollow tube which was rolled from a strip of metal.
