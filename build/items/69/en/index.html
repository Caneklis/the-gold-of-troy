A pin

with a flat cylindrical head decorated with rosettes and spirals

Inv. Аар 240, Бз 46, П 88, О 6134
Material: Gold; weight 5.80 g
Technique: Forging, soldering, drawing
Size: Overall height 5.78 cm.;,height of head 2.11 cm;,maximum width 2.36 cm.;,diameter of cylinder 1.55 cm.; diameter of wire 0.12 cm, lengyj of wire 0.12cm

A pin with a sharpened point of round wire and a head of complex form. The central part of the head consists of a flattened cylinder, rolled from thin plate, with convex disks soldered to either side. Around the edge of each disk is soldered a thin, round wire which encloses a filigree rosette of eleven petals.
