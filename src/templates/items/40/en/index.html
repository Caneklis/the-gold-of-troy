A string of beads and pendants of various shapes. There are very few minute beads and there are more large beads, including smooth and toothed round beads; square disks, barrel-shaped ribbed; and hexagonal truncated biconical beads; square beads with incisions on the sides; and tubular two-winged beads. There are two large barrel-shaped ribbed beads, two tubular tetrahedral beads, and one spindle-shaped bead. Also, there are two mushroom-shaped pendants and one rod-shaped pendant with incisions on its lower end. Some beads are soiled and slightly bent. Some beads are fused together.
