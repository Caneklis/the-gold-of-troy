A basket-shaped earring consisting of a bent plate with vertical incisions resembling 16 soldered wires. The upper front edge of the earring is decorated with applique ornamentation that consists of three soldered rosettes. These are made of two concentric wire rings, the spaces in the middle being filled with granulation. The ends of five double chains with idol-shaped pendants are threaded through the rings.
