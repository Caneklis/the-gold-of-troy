A basket-shaped earring consisting of 17 vertical wires soldered together, the central one flattened and approximately twice as wide as the rest.

On the front of the earring at the top is an applique decoration of three horizontal wires, between which are two rows of granulation.

A flat, narrow rectangular bar is soldered to the lower part of the earring, Six adjoining rings made of flat wire are soldered to the lower edge of the bar.
