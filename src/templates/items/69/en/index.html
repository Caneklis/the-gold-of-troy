A pin with a sharpened point of round wire and a head of complex form.

The central part of the head consists of a flattened cylinder, rolled from thin plate, with convex disks soldered to either side. Around the edge of each disk is soldered a thin, round wire which encloses a filigree rosette of eleven petals.
