A crescent-shaped earring consisting of a single piece made from a solid, compressed wire, thicker in the center and beaten out into a rounded hook ending in a wire curving along the contour of the earring. The crescent itself has three facets; the upper face is slightly concave. The ribs are decorated with rows of granulation. The side faces are decorated with arc-shaped rows of granulation that echo the contour of the crescent - eight on each side. At the end, the crescent is decorated with disks set on the round end of the crescent; the disk in the center is round with smooth edges.
