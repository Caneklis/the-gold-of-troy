Spherical vessel with a short cylindrical neck and flatly everted outer rim. To form the rim, the upper part of the neck was first turned outwards and then folded back inside and rolled in. The neck abruptly expands into a spherical body without a pronounced base. Around the central part of the circumference of the body are distinct traces of a three-tier frieze consisting of vertically extended rhombuses. In most cases the outlines of the rhombuses are defined by very fine horizontal scratches filled with a dark material.
