A basket-shaped earring made of a bent rectangular plate divided by vertical incisions into three ornamental zones. The central part of the upper edge of the front is hammered into a hook with a triangular base that extends into a long rectangular wire, curving beyond the back part of the earring. A flat trapezoidal bar with five perforations punched from the back is soldered by its long edge to the lower part of the earring. The front and back faces of the bar are decorated with a latticework ornamentation.
