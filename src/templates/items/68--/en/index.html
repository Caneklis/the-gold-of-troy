A pin with a sharpened shaft made of round wire, the upper part of which is flattened and secures a flat rectangular plate that is placed horizontally in relation to the wire. Six miniature, two-handled vessels with biconical bodies and disk-like rims on tall, cylindrical necks are soldered to the upper surface of the flattened wire. Each miniature vessel is made of seven elements soldered together.
