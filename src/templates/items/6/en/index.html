A long, narrow diadem band, worn on the forehead, with three holes at each rounded end. There is a line of dotted ornamentation around the edge of the band, except at the ends. The perforations at the ends of the band are pierced from the rear and the rims and borders originating on the ventral side project unevenly on the front. The dotted ornamentation and the diagonal lines which join the relief circles, as well as the circles themselves, were made from the reverse with a punch and a tool with a flat working edge.
