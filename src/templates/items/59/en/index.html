The bracelet is made of wide gold plate; wire rims are soldered to the uneven everted edges of the bracelet. The bracelet is decorated with slightly uneven lengthwise rows of spectacle-shaped double spirals made of thin wire, soldered on to the outside. The elements of the rosettes vary in size. The upper and lower rows of spirals are oriented in opposite directions and are slightly offset from one another.
