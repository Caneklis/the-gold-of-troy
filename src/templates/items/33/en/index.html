A crescent-shaped earring made from two partially soldered, three-faceted solid lobes with convex interior surfaces. One end is beaten out into a rounded hook which extends into a sharpened wire curving along the contour of the earring. The other end of the crescent features a horizontal row of granules and two flattened, six-sided buttons, one of which is set slightly higher than the other. The ribs of the lobes are decorated with rows of minute soldered granules.
