Sixty-four vertical chains made of double links are suspended from a long narrow band with three perforations at each rounded triangular end. Fifty short chains constitute the central part, and seven long chains frame it at each side. Rhomboid plaques with a raised, perforated central rib are hung on the chains at equal intervals every four links. All the chains have idol-shaped pendants. The idol-shaped pendants have semicircular tops, while the lower part widens out to the rounded base which has a nick in the center. The chains are directly attached to the diadem band without additional elements.
