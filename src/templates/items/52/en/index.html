A crescent-shaped earring consisting of a one-piece crescent made of a solid wire thicker in the middle. A rounded hook ending in a sharpened wire curves along the contour of the earring. The crescent has three facets; the upper part is slightly concave. The ribs are decorated with lengthwise rows of granulation; the sides are decorated with crosswise rows of granulation - eight on each. One end of the crescent is decorated with a compressed, six-sided button with a toothed disk above it.
