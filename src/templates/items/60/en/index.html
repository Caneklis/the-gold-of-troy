A basket-shaped earring made of a bent rectangular plate divided by vertical incisions into three ornamental zones. The central part of the upper edge at the front is beaten out into a hook with a triangular base that extends into a long rectangular wire, curving beyond the back of the earring. The upper parts of the front and back faces of the earring are decorated with horizontal rows of double wires, with rows of granulation between them. A flat trapezoidal bar with five perforations punched from the back is soldered by its long side to the lower part of the earring. Both the front and back faces of the bar are decorated with a latticework pattern.
