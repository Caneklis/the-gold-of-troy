A crescent-shaped item of jewelry made of six wires soldered together, thickening in the center and with one end beaten out into a rounded hook. The hook tapers to a wire, curving along the contour of the crescent. At the base of the hook three conical studs are soldered on to the crescent, arranged in a triangle. There are also rows of similar studs that differ in size and shape in rows across the body: two adjacent uneven rows, each of five studs, in the center of the crescent, and one row of three studs at its end.
