A basket-shaped earring consisting of 17 vertical wires soldered together.

On the front of the earring at the top is an applique decoration of three horizontal wires, between which are two rows of granulation, with 11 pellets in each row.

A flat, narrow rectangular bar is soldered to the lower part of the earring; six adjoined rings of flattened wire are soldered to its lower side.

The ends of five double chains with idol-shaped pendants are threaded into the rings.
